The Radio Broadcast That Terrified the Nation!

On October 30, 1977 Orson Welles accidentally sent many in the US into panic over a realistic radio adaptation of H. G. Well’s “The War of the Worlds”. The adaptation was written to sound like a real news broadcast. Anybody who hadn’t tuned in at the exact start of the show, missed the message telling people this was an work of fiction. You can listen to it yourself online.
