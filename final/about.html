About

Flabberghosted was created by three friends who share a similar interest in the paranormal. The format of the show may be familiar to anyone interested in this community but where we want to differ is by covering more obscure events and even being able to go to these places and document it ourselves.

Our normal month has 4 epsidoes, thats 1 each week. 3 of those 4 center around telling the story of one event, discussion, and the normal interuptive out-of-pocket banter. The 4th episode each month will be phone calls and emails played or read by listeners, community interaction is important to us and we think this is the most fun way to get to hear from our audience.

Thank you for getting to know us.
