FlabberGhosted:

A Paranormal Podcast

Prepare to be flabbergasted or... flabberghosted. We discuss the world of the paranormal, diving straight into the strange and mysterious. No stone will go unturned when we discuss aliens, ghosts, demons, and even listener stories. If the dark scares you, fear not for we dash in a light tone with comedy bouncing between the hosts.
