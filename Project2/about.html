About the Recipe

This simple and easy-to-make dish will have you putting down the phone for take-out. This recipe is made of chicken with vegetables coated in a garlic and ginger soy sauce with honey is exactly what you would want for a perfect combo of sweet and savory. This will only take 30 minutes total to make. 15 minutes prep time and then 15 minutes cooking time. The skill level for this is relatively low, making it east to cook for anybody at any skill tier.
